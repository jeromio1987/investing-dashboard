🎯 Dead Quiet Edge Framework
📖 Narrative
✓ One sentence — clear, specific, non-consensus
✓ Not mainstream — no CNBC breakout, no Bloomberg Alert
✓ Expanding — fundamentals growing, not shrinking
⚡ Catalyst
✓ Dated — within 60 days, not "sometime this year"
✓ Binary — pass/fail, yes/no, contract/no contract
✓ Underpriced — market not pricing full probability
🌊 Float
✓ Under 30M shares — small order moves the price
✓ 2 analysts or fewer — Goldman not watching
✓ Institutional <40% — retail vacuum available
🔇 Vacuum
✓ No Bloomberg Alert — last 30 days clean
✓ Volume below avg — crowd not here yet
✓ Price not reacted — catalyst not priced in
